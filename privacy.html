"Lipfit" Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

Contact: service@butof.com

English

"Lipfit" respects your privacy. This policy explains how the "Lipfit" mobile app handles information when you use it to try on lipstick shades with a live camera or a photo from your library.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app itself does not collect, upload, sell, or share your photos or personal data. It does not include account registration, advertising, third-party analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Camera Access

"Lipfit" requests camera access only to show a live preview for real-time lipstick try-on. Camera frames are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

You can change or revoke camera permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Photo Library Access

"Lipfit" requests access to your photo library so you can select a photo for lipstick try-on and save try-on results to your library. Photo processing happens locally on your device. Your photos are not uploaded to our servers.

If you grant limited photo access, the app can only work with the photos you selected. You can change or revoke photo permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

Local App Data

The app may store your preferences locally on your device — such as language setting, selected brand, and shade. This data is not uploaded to us.

Contact by Email

If you contact us by email, we may receive your email address and the content of your message. We use this information only to respond to your support request.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because the app does not upload your photos or app usage data to our servers, there is no server-side data for us to delete. You can remove local app data by deleting the app from your device. Try-on photos saved by the app are stored in your system photo library and can be managed through the Photos app.

Children's Privacy

"Lipfit" is not designed to collect personal information from children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date on this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at service@butof.com.

中文

"Lipfit 口红试色" 尊重你的隐私。本政策说明 "Lipfit 口红试色" App 在帮助你通过实时相机或相册照片试色口红时如何处理信息。

我们不会收集的信息

App 本身不会收集、上传、出售或共享你的照片或个人数据。App 不包含账号注册、广告、第三方分析或跟踪 SDK。

相机访问

"Lipfit" 仅在你授权后访问相机，用于显示实时预览以进行口红试色。相机画面在你的设备本地处理，不会上传到我们的服务器。

你可以随时在 iOS 系统设置中修改或撤销相机权限。

相册访问

"Lipfit 口红试色" 仅在你授权后访问相册，用于选取照片进行试色，以及将试色结果保存到你的相册。照片处理过程在你的设备本地完成，不会上传到我们的服务器。

如果你授予的是受限相册访问权限，App 只能处理你选择的照片。你可以随时在 iOS 系统设置中修改或撤销相册权限。

本地应用数据

App 可能会在设备本地保存你的偏好设置，如语言设置、所选品牌和色号。这些数据不会上传给我们。

通过邮件联系我们

如果你主动通过邮件联系我们，我们可能会收到你的邮箱地址和邮件内容。我们仅将这些信息用于回复你的支持请求。

数据保留与删除

由于 App 不会把你的照片或使用数据上传到我们的服务器，因此我们没有服务器端数据需要删除。你可以通过删除 App 来移除本地应用数据。App 保存的试色照片存储在系统相册中，可以通过照片 App 管理。

儿童隐私

"Lipfit 口红试色" 并非为了收集儿童个人信息而设计。App 不会有意收集任何人的个人信息。

政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。如有更新，我们会修改本页面的生效日期。

联系我们

如对本隐私政策有任何问题，请通过 service@butof.com 联系我们。
