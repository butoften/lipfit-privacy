About Camera and Photo Access

"Lipfit" uses system camera and photo permissions to provide live try-on and photo try-on locally on your device. If the camera preview is not showing or photo selection fails, please check whether camera and photo permissions are enabled in iOS Settings.

About Shade Accuracy

Lip shades shown in "Lipfit" are color-calibrated based on official brand swatch samples and published digital color values. The displayed result may vary depending on your device screen, lighting conditions, and individual lip tone.

关于相机和相册权限

"Lipfit" 使用系统相机和相册权限在设备本地提供实时试色和照片试色功能。如果相机预览未显示或照片选取失败，请在 iOS 系统设置中检查相机和相册权限是否已开启。

关于色号准确度

"Lipfit" 中显示的口红色号基于品牌官方色卡样本和公开数字色值进行颜色校准。实际显示效果可能因设备屏幕、光线环境和个人唇色不同而有所差异。
